WristSlots Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 27, 2025

Minimal by Design

WristSlots is a fun, offline slot machine game for Apple Watch. We do not collect, store, or transmit any user data.

No Data Collection

We don’t collect personal information, location data, device identifiers, or gameplay analytics. Everything stays on your watch.

Local Storage Only

Your coin balance and game settings (like mute or difficulty) are saved locally on the watch using Apple’s secure on-device storage (`AppStorage`).

No Internet Access

WristSlots works entirely offline. The app does not connect to the internet, so your data never leaves your device.

No Third‑Party Code

There are no ads, no SDKs, and no trackers. We don’t use analytics libraries or crash reporters.

Children’s Privacy

WristSlots does not knowingly collect data from anyone—children or otherwise. It’s a simple game, that’s it.
